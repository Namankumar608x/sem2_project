Alex De Minaur Ends Joao Fonseca Challenge, Alexander Zverev Marches On In Miami Open



Australia's Alex de Minaur ended Joao Fonseca's challenge at the Miami Open, winning 5-7, 7-5, 6-3 in a thrilling match. The Hard Rock Stadium transformed into a mini-Maracana with Brazilian flags and chants. Fonseca's ultra-attacking tennis tested De Minaur, but the Aussie handled both the powerful forehands and the partisan crowd, securing victory in a gripping contest.
