**David Warner to Lead Karachi Kings in PSL 2025** Former Australia batter David Warner will captain Karachi Kings in the upcoming 10th edition of the Pakistan Super League (PSL) from 11 April to 18 May. Warner replaces Pakistan Test captain Shan Masood and will be making his PSL debut. The 38-year-old, who led Sydney Thunder to the 2025 Big Bash League final, expressed excitement about the role. Warner, previously banned from leadership due to a ball-tampering scandal, has played 110 T20Is for Australia, scoring 3,277 runs.

Edwards

**Charlotte Edwards is the Ideal Candidate for England's Head Coach Role** Former England batter Lydia Greenway believes Charlotte Edwards is the only candidate England should consider for the women's team head coach position. With Jon Lewis and Heather Knight removed after England's disastrous 16-0 Ashes defeat, Greenway feels Edwards' experience, having led Mumbai Indians to two WPL titles and winning multiple domestic trophies, makes her the perfect fit. Edwards, a former England captain, has also been appointed head coach of Hampshire for 2025.
