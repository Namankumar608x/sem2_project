Ashutosh Sharma, Once Battling Depression After Being Dropped By IPL Coach, Now The Next 'Chasemaster'



Ashutosh Sharma powered Delhi Capitals to a thrilling IPL 2025 win over Lucknow Super Giants, smashing 66 off 31 balls. Walking in at 65/5, he anchored the chase of 210, finishing with three balls to spare. The 26-year-old has excelled in dramatic run chases, previously shining for Punjab Kings. His rise is remarkable, overcoming depression after being brutally dropped by a former coach.
