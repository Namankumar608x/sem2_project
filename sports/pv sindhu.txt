Swiss Open: PV Sindhu Fails In 1st Round, Kidambi Srikanth Moves To Pre-Quarterfinals


Two-time Olympic medalist PV Sindhu suffered a first-round exit at the Swiss Open, losing to world No. 31 Julie Jakobsen 21-17, 21-19. This marks her third consecutive first-round loss on the BWF World Tour in 2024. Ranked world No. 17, Sindhu had earlier reached the Indian Open quarterfinals but failed to progress past round one in the Indonesia Masters, All England Open, and now the Swiss Open.
