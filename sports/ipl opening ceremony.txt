IPL 2025 Opening Ceremony Highlights: SRK, Shreya Ghoshal, Disha Patani, Karan Aujla Grip Eden Gardens As League Turns 18


The 18th edition of the Indian Premier League was declared open with an elaborate ceremony at the Eden Gardens ahead of the curtain-raiser between holders Kolkata Knight Riders and Royal Challengers Bengaluru on Saturday.

Bollywood megastar Shah Rukh Khan headlined the opening ceremony as he set the stage with a monologue to throw the celebrations open.

Shreya Ghoshal, Disha Patani and Karan Aujla thrilled the fans with their cultural stylings before Shah Rukh Khan shared a few words with RCB star Virat Kohli and KKR starlet Rinku Singh as the a-listers danced to Bollywood tunes.
